Crystal Collector


To win, you must make your total score equal to the target number given at the begining of the game.

The value of each of the crystals below are unknown until you click on them. Each crystal will add a specific amount of points to your total score with each click.

If your total score goes above the target number, you lose.

Each time the game restarts, the values of each crystal will change.
